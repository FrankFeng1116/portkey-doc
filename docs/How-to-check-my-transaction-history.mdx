---
title: How to check my transaction history?
---

All your transactions, including token/NFT transfers and social recovery activities, are securely recorded on the blockchain. To view your transaction history, simply tap the time icon on the Portkey home screen.

<img src="/img/transaction-history.png" />

Click on any record to see its details, such as the date, status, transaction ID, and fees. For more in-depth information, click "View on Explorer" at the bottom, which will take you to the transaction details page on aelfscan. You can check additional data like block info and logs there.
