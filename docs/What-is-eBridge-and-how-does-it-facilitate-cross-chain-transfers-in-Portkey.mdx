---
title: What is eBridge and how does it facilitate cross-chain transfers in Portkey?
---

[eBridge](https://ebridge.exchange/bridge "") is a decentralised cross-chain bridge dedicated to providing the aelf community and users with a seamless and cost-effective cross-chain experience. It facilitates the effortless transfer of leading tokens like ELF, ETH, BNB, USDC, USDT, DAI, and SGR across major networksâ€”aelf, Ethereum, BNB Smart Chain (BSC), and Base.

Seamlessly integrated into Portkey, eBridge allows users to easily send and receive assets across various networks securely at low costs. With its enhanced security, eBridge safely facilitates sending and receiving ETH, BNB, USDC, and DAI as well as receiving USDT, ELF, and SGR for large volumes.

### Sending assets

With eBridge, Portkey users can send ETH, BNB, USDC, and DAI to networks including Ethereum, BSC, and Base. Simply click "Send", enter the recipient's address, select the target network, and complete the transaction. For a detailed guide, check out the step-by-step [asset sending tutorial](/docs/How-to-send-assets "").

### Receiving assets

With eBridge, Portkey users can receive ETH, BNB, USDC, and DAI or receive USDT, ELF, and SGR  with a value of $20,000 or more from networks such as Ethereum, BSC, and Base. Click "Receive", select a token and the source network, connect your wallet to the source network, and confirm the transaction. Your Portkey account will receive the tokens shortly thereafter. For a detailed guide, check out the step-by-step [asset receiving tutorial](/docs/How-to-receive-assets "").
