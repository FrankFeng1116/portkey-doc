---
title: What is ETransfer and how does it facilitate cross-chain transfers in Portkey?
---

[ETransfer](https://etransfer.exchange/ "") is a specialised cross-chain bridge designed for seamless token transfers between the aelf ecosystem and other popular networks such as Ethereum, Solana, and TON. It addresses the critical need for interoperability across different blockchain ecosystems, enabling users to effortlessly bridge and manage their assets.

Seamlessly integrated into Portkey, ETransfer allows users to send and receive assets across various networks with just a few clicks. ETransfer makes transferring small volumes of digital assets like USDT, ELF, and SGR fast, straightforward, and hassle-free, removing the need to connect multiple wallets.

### Sending assets

With ETransfer, Portkey users can send USDT, ELF, and SGR to networks including Ethereum, Tron, BNB Smart Chain (BSC), Arbitrum One, Solana, Polygon, Optimism, AVAX C-Chain, and TON.

Simply click "Send", enter the recipient's address, select the target network, and complete the transaction. For a detailed guide, check out the step-by-step [asset sending tutorial](/docs/How-to-send-assets "").

### Receiving assets

With ETransfer, Portkey users can receive USDT, ELF, and SGR with a value of less than $20,000 from networks including Ethereum, Tron, BNB Smart Chain (BSC), Arbitrum One, Solana, Polygon, Optimism, AVAX C-Chain, and TON.

Click "Receive", select a token, and you'll be provided with an address for the transfer. Your Portkey account will receive the tokens shortly thereafter. For a detailed guide, check out the step-by-step [asset receiving tutorial](/docs/How-to-receive-assets "").
