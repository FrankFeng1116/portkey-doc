---
title: What is aelf?
---

* aelf is a high-performance public blockchain with a single-chain TPS as high as 35,000. Its "MainChain and multiple SideChains" structure ensures almost infinite scalability. For developers, the building experience on aelf is smooth and flexible since aelf provides comprehensive tools and different fee models to cater for various needs.
* Also, aelf blockchain is written in C# which is a language commonly used in the gaming industry. This is a great advantage for realising Portkey's goal to migrate Web2 games to Web3.
